Capstone: From Bricks to Bucks

This project examined sales patterns of LEGO sets to predict their secondary market prices using machine learning. It compared licensed vs. original sets and analyzed trends in quantity, complexity, and price over time. Key steps included time-series analysis, clustering, and predictive modeling using linear regression, elastic net, random forest, and SVM. Results showed that historical price data is a strong predictor of value, though models excluding it sometimes performed better.
Tools used: R (tidyverse, caret, plotly, shiny, randomForest, glmnet, DT, e1071), Quarto
